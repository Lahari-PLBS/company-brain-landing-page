Hospital Code of Conduct & Policies
===================================
1. Patient Confidentiality (HIPAA): All staff must strictly safeguard patient medical information. Sharing files or patient data with unauthorized parties is a zero-tolerance offense.
2. Safety & Hygiene: Standard sterilization protocols must be followed before entering any operating theater. Hand sanitization is mandatory at all checkpoints.
3. Anti-Harassment: We promote a safe, inclusive, and respectful workspace. Any report of harassment or discrimination will be investigated immediately by HR.
4. Dress Code: All staff members must wear their official badges prominently. Medical scrubs must be clean and conform to department specifications.
5. Work Hours: Shift changes must be signed off by the incoming supervisor. No staff should leave their post until their relief arrives.
